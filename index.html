Cardiovascular Equipment

Cardiovascular equipment in a fitness studio includes treadmills, stationary bikes, elliptical trainers, and rowing machines. These machines are designed to elevate heart rate, improve endurance, and burn calories, making them essential for cardio workouts. Users can customize their workouts by adjusting speed, resistance, and incline levels on these machines, catering to various fitness levels. Cardiovascular equipment plays a crucial role in promoting cardiovascular health and overall fitness, making them a staple in any well-equipped fitness studio.
